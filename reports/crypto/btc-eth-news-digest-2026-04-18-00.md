Sources: [BLS CPI](https://www.bls.gov/news.release/cpi.htm?lv=true), [BLS PPI](https://www.bls.gov/news.release/ppi.nr0.htm).

My judgment is that BTC has the cleaner `intraday to 1-3 session` setup, while ETH needs either stronger ETF breadth or a broader risk-on extension across equities to outperform materially. Coverage is not thin this run because the dominant catalyst is real and current; it is simply macro-led rather than crypto-native.
