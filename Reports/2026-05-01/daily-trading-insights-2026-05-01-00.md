# Daily Trading Insights - 2026-05-01

> [!abstract] Report Scope
> English U.S. trading intelligence note for Friday, 2026-05-01, plus the next 1-3 U.S. sessions. A separate Traditional Chinese translation is saved as `daily-trading-insights-2026-05-01-00-zh-tw.md`.

## Detailed Summary

The market enters Friday, 2026-05-01 with a constructive but narrow tape. The bullish side is easy to identify: Apple beat fiscal Q2 expectations with $111.2B revenue and $2.01 EPS, Alphabet delivered one of the strongest AI/cloud read-throughs, and Microsoft/Amazon/Meta all confirmed large-scale AI and cloud demand. The bearish side is macro: the Fed held rates at 3.50%-3.75% with an unusually divided vote, March PCE reaccelerated to 3.5% y/y, Q1 PCE inflation ran 4.5% annualized, and oil remains elevated because Hormuz disruption is still unresolved.

Today's trading map is therefore not "buy everything." It is "buy quality earnings if DXY and 2Y yields cooperate; hedge with energy/gold if inflation pressure reappears." ISM Manufacturing at 10:00 a.m. ET is the immediate macro catalyst, especially the prices-paid component. The next few sessions bring trade balance, ISM Services, JOLTS on 2026-05-05, ADP on 2026-05-06, productivity on 2026-05-07, and payrolls on 2026-05-08.
