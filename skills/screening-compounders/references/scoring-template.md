# Scoring Template

Score only companies that pass all prior steps.

## Recommended weights

- `ROCE history`: 4.0
- `financial robustness`: 2.0
- `moat durability / convergent evolution`: 2.0
- `owner alignment and capital allocation discipline`: 1.0
- `valuation context`: 1.0

Total possible score: `10`

## Interpretation

- `9.0-10.0`: exceptional long-term compounder candidate
- `8.0-8.9`: high-quality candidate
- `7.0-7.9`: acceptable but with visible caveats
- `<7.0`: do not include in the final list unless the sector is unusually weak and the limitation is explained

## Notes

- ROCE should dominate.
- A cheap valuation cannot offset mediocre quality.
- A great business can still rank lower if current valuation is clearly stretched versus its own history.
