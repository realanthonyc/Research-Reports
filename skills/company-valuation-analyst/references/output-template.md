# Output Template

Use this structure for the final memo unless the user asks for another format.

## Snapshot

- Ticker / exchange / analysis date
- Current price and approximate market cap
- Main catalysts in the next 3-12 months
- One-line setup summary

## Business Model In Plain Language

- Explain what the company actually owns or sells.
- Say whether value depends mainly on segments, installed base, cycle, assets, brand, platform, one catalyst, or one regulatory / execution event.

## What Drives Value

- Lead program(s)
- Segment economics or strategic assets
- Growth / margin / cycle / catalyst path
- Monetization route and what has to go right
- Key assumptions that matter most

## Balance Sheet And Dilution

- Cash
- Debt
- Liquidity
- Runway if relevant
- Financing overhang
- Per-share impact of likely dilution or buyback

## Scenario Valuation Table

Show `bear / base / bull` with:

- probability
- value before dilution
- dilution percent
- value after dilution
- implied per-share value if the share count is known
- method used

## What The Market Is Pricing In Now

- Explain whether the market is already pricing in a high-success outcome.
- If a sharp rally happened, discuss whether sentiment, squeeze, cycle enthusiasm, or multiple expansion are amplifying price beyond base-case value.

## Verdict

- One sentence only.

## Critical Unknowns

- Data gaps
- Fragile assumptions
- What could move the fair value range materially

## What To Watch Next

- Next catalyst
- Financing risk
- Evidence that would change the probability or peak-sales assumption
