# Framework Selection

Pick the comparison criteria that fit the industry instead of using one universal template.

## Stable Compounders

Prioritize:

- recurring revenue quality
- pricing power
- retention / customer stickiness
- margin durability
- capital allocation
- valuation relative to quality

## Cyclicals

Prioritize:

- cycle position
- balance sheet strength
- cost position
- asset quality
- mid-cycle earnings power
- downside survivability

## Asset-Heavy / Infrastructure / Financial-like Balance Sheet Stories

Prioritize:

- asset quality
- liability structure
- leverage
- liquidity
- return on capital
- replacement cost or NAV

## High-Growth Platform / Software / Network Effects

Prioritize:

- TAM realism
- customer acquisition efficiency
- gross margin structure
- retention / engagement / ecosystem strength
- path to durable margin
- valuation versus durability

## Turnarounds / Special Situations

Prioritize:

- downside floor
- time to self-funding
- refinancing risk
- management credibility
- catalysts
- gap between current price and recovery value

## Event-Driven / Binary Outcomes

Prioritize:

- probability-weighted upside
- downside floor
- financing risk
- catalyst timing
- whether the market already priced in success

## Minimum Rule

Before ranking, write 2-4 lines explaining why those criteria matter more than the obvious alternatives for this specific industry.
